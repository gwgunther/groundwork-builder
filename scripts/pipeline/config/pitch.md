# Pitch boilerplate

This is the **only** place in the codebase where pre-written user-facing copy
lives. It is OUR pitch (Groundwork) to the practice owner — not site copy for
the practice's own website. Site copy is exclusively scraped, AI-rewritten, or
AI-written from scratch with no template priming.

The fields below are rendered into `_pipeline/one-pager.html` and the closing
section of `_pipeline/external-report.html`. Edit here when the pitch evolves.

---

headline: We rebuild dental practice websites from scratch in under an hour.

subheadline: One automated pipeline. Brand-direction, design system, copy, and a deployable site — sourced from your existing site and tuned to your practice.

valueProps:
  - title: Brand-first, not template-first
    body: Every site starts with a fresh palette, typography, and voice that is specific to your practice — never picked from a template library.
  - title: Real content, not lorem ipsum
    body: We pull your existing copy verbatim where it's strong, and AI-write what's missing using your practice's own context — never generic boilerplate.
  - title: Editable Astro project, not a CMS lock-in
    body: You receive the full source — version-controlled, hostable anywhere. No monthly platform fees, no migration cost when you outgrow us.
  - title: A starting point, not the finish line
    body: This is groundwork. You'll have a clear list of what's missing, what we recommend next, and what would benefit from a deeper conversation.

engagement:
  whatYouGet:
    - A complete redesign as a deployable Astro project
    - A detailed audit of your current site (this report)
    - A brand direction document with palette, typography, voice
    - A list of action items prioritized by impact
  nextStep: Reach out to schedule a 30-minute walkthrough of this report and discuss what an engagement could look like.
