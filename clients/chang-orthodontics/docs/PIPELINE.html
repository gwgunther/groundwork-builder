Silver

AI-extracts bronze into a structured PracticeData object. Three content tiers: structured fields (practice, doctors, services, hours) capture canonical data; differentiators[] captures short labels for "why us" facts; additionalContent[] rescues verbatim prose blocks (pull-quotes, philosophy paragraphs, blog bodies). Visual/design data is OUT OF SCOPE — colors, fonts, mood belong to Design Extract.

InBronze pages — top 8 by relevance (home, about, contact, doctor, team, services). Selection is for prompt-size only; ALL distinctive content within those pages is preserved across the three tiers.

Catch-all rescue. Each item: { type, title, content, source }. Captures pull-quotes, philosophy paragraphs, "Welcome To" intros, office descriptions, technology copy, mission statements, blog bodies, taglines — anything distinctive that didn't fit a structured field.
